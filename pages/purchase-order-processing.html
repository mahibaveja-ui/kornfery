Purchase Order Processing Services

Smart Line Core streamlines sales order management by digitizing and processing purchase orders with speed and precision. From paper and fax to email and EDI, our advanced data capture system eliminates manual errors and improves turnaround time—empowering your team to focus on core operations while enhancing customer satisfaction.

How Our Process Works

  • Orders received via paper, fax, email, or EDI are routed to our secure facility.
  • Documents are digitized using advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology.
  • Intelligent software extracts critical data like order details, customer info, and delivery schedule.
  • Data is validated against existing systems for accuracy.
  • Converted outputs are delivered in your preferred format (CSV, Excel, HTML, etc.).
  • Scanned copies are stored in our cloud-based system for quick retrieval.
  • Orders are auto-routed to relevant departments for approval or action.

Accuracy & Speed

Reduce manual entry errors and process orders faster with automated data capture and validation.

Secure Handling

PCI-compliant facilities ensure sensitive financial data is processed and stored securely.

Cloud-Based Document Access

Every purchase order processed is stored securely in our cloud system. Documents are categorized by customer, order number, or date—making retrieval quick and efficient, without piles of paper.
