Smart Line Core provides expert Managed Service Provider (MSP) and Vendor Management System (VMS) solutions to efficiently manage your contingent workforce. From recruitment to onboarding, timesheets to billing, our team ensures seamless processes, vendor-neutral management, and optimal performance.

How Staffing Worked Traditionally

  • Hiring manager approvals and searching for staffing agencies manually.
  • Receiving resumes, coordinating interviews, and issuing purchase orders.
  • Fragmented processes led to inefficiencies, delays, and missed opportunities.
  • Agency sales reps operated individually without centralized oversight.

Managed Services Provider (MSP)

MSPs manage contingent workforce programs on behalf of clients, acting as a centralized and unbiased interface between hiring managers and staffing agencies. We handle requisition distribution, candidate submissions, interview scheduling, onboarding, timesheets, approvals, billing, and vendor performance tracking.

By leveraging a VMS platform, our MSP services automate and streamline processes, reduce errors, and maintain vendor neutrality, giving clients control, transparency, and efficiency.

Vendor Management System (VMS)

VMS is the software solution that enables MSPs to efficiently run contingent workforce programs. It automates requisition distribution, candidate tracking, interview management, billing, and timesheet collection.

  • Integration with client HR systems for seamless operations.
  • Automated metrics tracking, vendor performance scorecards.
  • Compliance with client policies and legal regulations.

History of VMS & MSP

Initially, large companies managed temp workers through individual agencies, which was inefficient and inconsistent. In the mid-90s, MSPs emerged to centralize and streamline these processes, reduce costs, and manage workforce programs effectively.

Today, VMS platforms complement MSP services by providing online management tools. Clients select MSPs for expertise, while VMS software provides process automation and analytics.

Centralized Management

MSP provides a single point of control for all staffing and vendor interactions, increasing efficiency.

Automated Processes

VMS ensures automated tracking, approvals, and reporting, reducing manual errors and delays.

Vendor Neutrality

MSP acts as a fair intermediary, ensuring equal treatment of all staffing vendors.
